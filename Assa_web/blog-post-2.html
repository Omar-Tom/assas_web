Winging It: The Perfect Crispy Wing

Explore how to make the crispiest wings at home!

The Secret to Crispy Wings

Whether you're cooking for a crowd or just craving a delicious snack, crispy wings are a must-try. The secret to achieving that perfect crunch lies in the right combination of ingredients and cooking techniques. In this post, we’ll reveal the steps for making wings that are golden brown on the outside and tender on the inside.

The Importance of Drying the Wings

Before you even think about seasoning your wings, you must start with the right prep. Drying your wings with a paper towel removes excess moisture that can cause sogginess when cooked. The dryer the wings, the crispier the result.

Use Cornstarch for the Ultimate Crunch

One of the key ingredients for crispy wings is cornstarch. Adding cornstarch to your seasoning mix creates a thin, crispy crust that locks in the moisture of the meat. It’s an essential part of making wings that are perfectly crisp every time!

"The perfect wing should be crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside. Cornstarch is the secret ingredient!"

- Wing Enthusiast

Frying Tips for Crispy Wings

Frying wings is one of the most popular methods for cooking them. To get crispy wings, ensure your oil is at the right temperature – around 350°F (175°C). If the oil is too hot, the outside will burn before the inside cooks. Too cool, and the wings will be soggy. Fry in small batches for the best results!
